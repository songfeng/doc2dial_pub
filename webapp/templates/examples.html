Example 1: not in immediate proximity

In some cases, if a user changes an IBM serial number or email, they might temporarily lose access to their Box account. Refer to Login Errors for potential solutions.

Example 2: having only preconditions (second below) 

This error message appears in two scenarios:

1) You don't have an account provisioned with Box@IBM. You must create a Box@IBM account from a computer before gaining access to Box@IBM resources on your mobile device. For account setup instructions, refer to Use Box@IBM: Register for an Account.

2) You have incorrectly managed-app configurations loaded on your device.

Example 3: multiple solution points

If you don't have access to the IBM network, you can reset your w3id password using this external URL.

You can also have the password sent to your mobile device or IBM email. To have the password sent to your mobile device, your mobile number must be in your BluePages profile. 

Example 4: having only solutions (TBD)

problem: Transfer Ownership of Box Folders

When you leave IBM, your Box@IBM account automatically deactivates when your BluePages record goes inactive. Transfer ownership by changing the permission of a collaborator to owner of the shared folder in Box. Setting a collaborator as a co-owner doesn't transfer ownership and results with all collaborators losing access after you leave IBM. Refer to Transfer Folder Ownershipfor more information.

If you receive other errors when trying to log in to Box@IBM:

  1. Confirm you're using the correct URL to access Box@IBM: https://ibm.box.com.
  2. Clear your browser's cache and cookies. Refer to browser-specific instructions if needed: 
    • Firefoxor Safari: Clear browsing history and cookies
    • Chrome: Clear cache and cookies
    • Safari on iOS: Clear the history and cookies from Safari on your iPhone, iPad, or iPod touch
